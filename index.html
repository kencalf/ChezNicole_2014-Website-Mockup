Our Story

We have been in business for over 18 years; our original founders being Nicole Testa and her father Joe Cannatelli. Cynthia Herrick, Nicole's sister joined our team in 1996 and cut hair for the next 13 years. She helped build the business into what it is today. Joe eventually sold the hair side of the salon to Nicole and Cynthia's mother Madelyn. Sadly, in November of 2009 & April of 2010 we lost both Nicole & Cynthia to brain cancer. Madelyn and Kenny Testa, Nicole's late husband, now run the salon and honor Nicole and Cynthia's memory by continuing their mission of excellence and professional service in a friendly atmosphere.

Nicole (founder of the nail salon, on left) and Cynthia (stylist, on right). Two beautiful sisters and talented hair and nail technicians, the strongest links of Chez Nicole. Nicole and Cynthia passed away from brain cancer between 2009-2010. They will be missed dearly in the community and in the salon.

We strive for excellence in our profession and friendliness in our service. We listen to your wants and needs, and work on a one to one basis to achieve the look to fit your lifestyle. We believe in looking great and feeling great, and maintaining a positive attitude in our everyday life. We are committed to bringing out the best in you..
